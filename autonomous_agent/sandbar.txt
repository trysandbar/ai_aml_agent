Complete the Sandbar AML customer review workflow:

## Login Flow (Steps 1-8)
1. Navigate to https://app.dev.sandbar.ai
2. You should see a page with "Sign into your account" heading
3. Click the button that says "Sign in with Google" (it has the Google logo)
4. Wait for Google sign-in page to load
5. Find the email input field and type: {EMAIL}
6. Click the blue "Next" button
7. Wait for the password page to load (you'll see "Welcome" and your email)
8. Find the password input field and type: {PASSWORD}
9. Click the blue "Next" button
10. Wait 15 seconds - you may see 2FA prompt or be automatically logged in
11. If you see a 2FA page with title containing "2-Step Verification":
    - Wait another 20 seconds for automatic approval (don't click anything)
    - The page should automatically redirect after approval
12. After successful login, you should see a table with multiple tenant names (columns: Name, Slug, Created at)

## Tenant Selection (Steps 13-16)
13. Look at the table - it has columns: Name, Slug, Created at
14. Find the row where Name column says "Current"
15. Click anywhere on that row (the "Current" row)
16. Wait 3 seconds - you should now be at a URL containing "/current"

## Navigate to Customers (Steps 17-19)
17. You are now on the Sandbar dashboard (URL contains /current)
18. Look for a navigation link or button that says "Customers" - it might be in a sidebar on the left or in the top navigation
19. Click on the "Customers" link to navigate to the customers page
20. Wait 3 seconds for the customers page to load

## Verify Customers Page (Step 21)
21. You should see:
    - A table with columns: Name, Alerts, Onboarded date
    - Multiple customer rows (names like "Robert Hindman", "Robert Stetser", etc.)
    - Some customers have "—" in Alerts column, some have badges like "PEP"
    - A "Load more" button at the bottom

## Filter for Open Alerts (Steps 22-30)
22. Look for a button with 3 horizontal lines that looks like an inverted triangle, located above "Name" column header, to the left of the table
23. Click that horizontal lines button to open the filter panel
24. Wait 1 second for the filter panel to appear on the left side
25. In the filter panel, you should see options like: Name, Customer ID, Type, Onboarded date, Alert status, Alert type, etc.
26. Click on "Alert status" to expand its dropdown options
27. Wait 1 second for the dropdown to appear
28. You should now see 4 options: Open, Closed, Deprecated, No alerts
29. Click "Open" (the one with the red circle icon)
30. Click somewhere outside the filter panel to close it
31. Wait 2 seconds - you should see "Alert status: Open" badge at the top and the table is now filtered

## Load More Customers (Steps 32-36)
32. Scroll to the bottom of the customer table
33. Click the "Load more" button
34. Wait 2 seconds, then click "Load more" again
35. Wait 2 seconds, then click "Load more" one final time (3 clicks total)
36. You should now see 30-40 customers in the table

## Find Customer to Review (Steps 37-40)
37. Look carefully at the customer table
38. Pick any customer that has badges or text in the "Alerts" column (NOT just "—")
39. Examples of customers to click:
    - Customer with "PEP" badge
    - Customer with "SANCTIONS" badge
    - Customer with numbered badges like "2"
40. Remember this customer's name

## Click Customer (Steps 41-42)
41. Click on the row of the customer you identified (click on their name)
42. Wait 5 seconds for the customer detail page to load

## Read AI Summary (Steps 43-46)
43. On the customer page, look for a section with heading "AI Summary"
44. Next to "AI Summary" you should see a colored badge
45. The badge will say one of these:
    - "Clear" (green) = NOT suspicious
    - "Review" (yellow/orange) = Needs review
    - "Investigate" (red) = Suspicious
46. Remember what the badge says

## Determine Decision (Step 47)
47. Based on what the AI Summary badge said:
    - If badge was "Clear" → Decision: NO MATCH
    - If badge was "Review" → Decision: MATCH
    - If badge was "Investigate" → Decision: MATCH

## Report Results (Steps 48-49)
48. Use evaluate() to check the current URL and page title
49. Describe what you accomplished:
    - What was the customer name you reviewed?
    - What did the AI Summary badge say?
    - What decision did you determine (MATCH or NO MATCH)?
    - What is the current URL?

GOAL_ACHIEVED when you complete step 49 and can report:
- Successfully logged in and selected Current tenant
- Navigated to customers page
- Filtered for Open alerts
- Loaded more customers
- Found and clicked a customer without PEP badge
- Read their AI Summary badge
- Determined the appropriate decision (MATCH or NO MATCH)
