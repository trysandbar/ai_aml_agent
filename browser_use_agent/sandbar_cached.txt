Complete the Sandbar AML customer review workflow (using cached session):

3. Scroll down 0.3 pages to view the tenant table

## Navigate to Customers (Steps 9-13)
9. You are now on the Sandbar dashboard (URL contains /current)
10. Scroll down 0.2 pages

## Verify Customers Page (Step 15)
15. Scroll down 0.3 pages to view customer table
16. Wait 2 seconds
17. You should see:

    - Multiple customer rows (names like "Robert Hindman", "Robert Stetser", etc.)

## Filter for Open Alerts (Steps 18-33)
18. Scroll up 0.2 pages to see the top of the table
19. Wait 2 seconds for the page to fully load
20. Look for a button with 3 horizontal lines that looks like an inverted triangle, located above "Name" column header, to the left of the table
21. Click that horizontal lines button to open the filter panel
22. Wait 3 seconds for the filter panel to appear on the left side
23. Wait 1 more second to ensure all filter options are loaded
24. Scroll down 0.1 pages to view filter options
25. Wait 1 second
26. In the filter panel, you should see options like: Name, Customer ID, Type, Onboarded date, Alert status, Alert type, etc.
27. Find the "Alert status" field in the filter panel
28. Click on the "Alert status" field itself (NOT any button next to it) to expand its dropdown options
29. Wait 2 seconds for the dropdown menu to fully appear
30. You should now see a dropdown menu with 4 options: Open, Closed, Deprecated, No alerts
31. Each option has an icon - look for "Open" which has a red circle icon next to it
32. Click on the "Open" option in the dropdown menu (the one with the red circle icon)
33. Wait 2 seconds
34. Click somewhere outside the filter panel to close it
35. Wait 3 seconds - you should see "Alert status: Open" badge appear at the top of the table and the table is now filtered to show only customers with open alerts

53. Scroll down all the way to the bottom of the customer table to see the last customers loaded

## Click Customer (Steps 62-64)
62. Click on the row of the customer you identified (click on their name)
63. Wait 5 seconds for the customer detail page to load
64. Scroll down 0.3 pages to view the page content
65. Wait 2 seconds for the page to fully render

## Read AI Summary (Steps 66-71)
66. Wait 2 seconds
67. Scroll down slowly 0.4 pages
68. On the customer page, look for a section with heading "AI Summary"
69. Next to "AI Summary" you should see a colored badge
70. The badge will say one of these:
    - "Clear" (green) = NOT suspicious
    - "Review" (yellow/orange) = Needs review
    - "Investigate" (red) = Suspicious
71. Remember what the badge says

## Determine Decision (Step 72)
72. Based on what the AI Summary badge said:
    - If badge was "Clear" → Decision: NO MATCH
    - If badge was "Review" → Decision: MATCH
    - If badge was "Investigate" → Decision: MATCH
