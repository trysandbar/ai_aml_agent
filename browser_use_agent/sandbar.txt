Complete the Sandbar AML customer review workflow:

2. Wait 4 seconds for the page to fully load

4. Wait 2 seconds
5. Scroll back up 0.3 pages
6. You should see a page with "Sign into your account" heading
7. Click the button that says "Sign in with Google" (it has the Google logo)
8. Wait 5 seconds for Google sign-in page to load
9. Scroll down 0.2 pages to view the full form
10. Wait 2 seconds
11. Find the email input field and type: {EMAIL}
12. Wait 1 second
13. Scroll down 0.1 pages
14. Click the blue "Next" button
15. Wait 4 seconds for the password page to load (you'll see "Welcome" and your email)
16. Scroll down 0.2 pages
17. Wait 2 seconds
18. Find the password input field and type: {PASSWORD}
19. Wait 1 second
20. Click the blue "Next" button
21. Wait 15 seconds - Google will either show 2FA or redirect directly to tenant page
22. Scroll down 0.2 pages to see what's on the page
23. Wait 2 seconds
24. If you see "2-Step Verification" heading or any 2FA prompts:
    - Scroll down 0.3 pages
    - Wait 10 seconds
    - Scroll up 0.2 pages
    - Wait another 30 seconds (human is clicking Yes on phone)
    - Wait another 20 seconds for redirect to complete
25. After redirect, you should now see a page with a table
26. The table should have multiple tenant names with columns: Name, Slug, Created at

## Tenant Selection (Steps 27-31)
27. Scroll down 0.3 pages to view the tenant table
28. Wait 2 seconds
29. Look at the table - it has columns: Name, Slug, Created at
30. Find the row where Name column says "Current"
31. Click anywhere on that row (the "Current" row)
32. Wait 4 seconds - you should now be at a URL containing "/current"

## Navigate to Customers (Steps 33-37)
33. You are now on the Sandbar dashboard (URL contains /current)
34. Scroll down 0.2 pages
35. Wait 1 second
36. Look for a navigation link or button that says "Customers" - it might be in a sidebar on the left or in the top navigation
37. Click on the "Customers" link to navigate to the customers page
38. Wait 4 seconds for the customers page to load

## Verify Customers Page (Step 39)
39. Scroll down 0.3 pages to view customer table
40. Wait 2 seconds
41. You should see:
    - A table with columns: Name, Alerts, Onboarded date
    - Multiple customer rows (names like "Robert Hindman", "Robert Stetser", etc.)
    - Some customers have "—" in Alerts column, some have badges like "PEP"
    - A "Load more" button at the bottom

## Filter for Open Alerts (Steps 42-57)
42. Scroll up 0.2 pages to see the top of the table
43. Wait 2 seconds for the page to fully load
44. Look for a button with 3 horizontal lines that looks like an inverted triangle, located above "Name" column header, to the left of the table
45. Click that horizontal lines button to open the filter panel
46. Wait 3 seconds for the filter panel to appear on the left side
47. Wait 1 more second to ensure all filter options are loaded
48. Scroll down 0.1 pages to view filter options
49. Wait 1 second
50. In the filter panel, you should see options like: Name, Customer ID, Type, Onboarded date, Alert status, Alert type, etc.
51. Find the "Alert status" field in the filter panel
52. Click on the "Alert status" field itself (NOT any button next to it) to expand its dropdown options
53. Wait 2 seconds for the dropdown menu to fully appear
54. You should now see a dropdown menu with 4 options: Open, Closed, Deprecated, No alerts
55. Each option has an icon - look for "Open" which has a red circle icon next to it
56. Click on the "Open" option in the dropdown menu (the one with the red circle icon)
57. Wait 2 seconds
58. Click somewhere outside the filter panel to close it
59. Wait 3 seconds - you should see "Alert status: Open" badge appear at the top of the table and the table is now filtered to show only customers with open alerts

## Find Customer to Review (Steps 77-84)
77. Scroll down all the way to the bottom of the customer table to see the last customers loaded
78. Wait 2 seconds
79. Scroll up 0.3 pages from the bottom to review customers near the end of the list
80. Wait 2 seconds to let the page stabilize
81. Look carefully at the customer table
82. Pick any customer that has badges or text in the "Alerts" column (NOT just "—")
83. Examples of customers to click:
    - Customer with "PEP" badge
    - Customer with "SANCTIONS" badge
    - Customer with numbered badges like "2"
84. Remember this customer's name
85. IMPORTANT: Pick a customer from the bottom half of the list, not from the top

## Click Customer (Steps 86-88)
86. Click on the row of the customer you identified (click on their name)
87. Wait 5 seconds for the customer detail page to load
88. Scroll down 0.3 pages to view the page content
89. Wait 2 seconds for the page to fully render

## Read AI Summary (Steps 90-95)
90. Wait 2 seconds
91. Scroll down slowly 0.4 pages
92. On the customer page, look for a section with heading "AI Summary"
93. Next to "AI Summary" you should see a colored badge
94. The badge will say one of these:
    - "Clear" (green) = NOT suspicious
    - "Review" (yellow/orange) = Needs review
    - "Investigate" (red) = Suspicious
95. Remember what the badge says

## Determine Decision (Step 96)
96. Based on what the AI Summary badge said:
    - If badge was "Clear" → Decision: NO MATCH
    - If badge was "Review" → Decision: MATCH
    - If badge was "Investigate" → Decision: MATCH

## Report Results (Steps 97-98)
97. Use evaluate() to check the current URL and page title
98. Describe what you accomplished:
    - What was the customer name you reviewed?
    - What did the AI Summary badge say?
    - What decision did you determine (MATCH or NO MATCH)?
    - What is the current URL?
